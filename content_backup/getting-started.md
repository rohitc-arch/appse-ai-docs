If you don’t have an account yet, you can visit [appse ai](https://workflow.appse.ai/) and finish the onboarding steps to sign up for absolutely free. This gives you access to core features to start building integrations right away.

When you're ready to unlock premium features & higher usage limits, check out our [pricing page](https://appse.ai/pricing/) to explore available plans.

## Onboarding to APPSe AI Platform

This guide walks you through the onboarding journey, from accessing the platform to successfully landing on your personalized dashboard.


### Step 1: Visit APPSe AI

Go to [appse ai](https://workflow.appse.ai/). You’ll have a choice between `Login` and `Register for free`. Since we are creating a new account, select `Register for free`


### Step 2: Add Your Email and Password

- Enter a valid Work Email
- Set up your Password. Ensure the password has at least 7 characters, including uppercase and lowercase letters, a number, and a special character.

Then click on `Sign up` to proceed. You will receive a verification email.

**Note**: You can close this window now if you want.


### Step 3: Follow the onboarding steps

- Once you click on the link, you will be redirected to the onboarding page
- Enter your first and last name, organization name, and phone number(optional).
- Fill up a few personalization questions, and you will be onboarded to the platform.

---

## Log in to the platform

If you want to log in to APPSe AI, visit [appse ai](https://workflow.appse.ai/) again and click on the `Log in` button this time.

---

## Forgot Password

If you can’t remember your password, you can reset it using your registered email. Follow the steps below to create a new password and log back into your account.
- In the login screen, look for the `Forgot password` link underneath the password field.
- Fill in your registered email. You will receive a verification link in your email inbox. Click on it.
- You will now have to set a new password. Once done, click on `Confirm new password`. And you’re done!
